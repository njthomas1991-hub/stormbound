Rules

Stormbound: A Battle of the Elements is a modern take on the traditional game 'Rock, Paper Scissors'.
You choose from the elements:
'water',
'fire',
'earth',
'air',
and 'lightning'.
Once you have chosen your element, the computer will randomly select its own element, after a count down of 3.

Elements

The winner is determined based on the following rules:

BEATS
Fire: is extinguished by Water,
Lightning: is grounded by Water,

Loses to:
Air: disperses Water,
Earth: blocks and absorbs Water

BEATS
Air: is consumed by Fire,
Earth: is melted by Fire,

Loses to:
Water: extinguishes Fire
Lightning: overwhelms Fire Fire: is extinguished by Water
Fire: is overwhelmed by Lightning

BEATS
Lightning: is absorbed by Earth,
Water: is blocked by Earth,

Loses to:
Air: erodes Earth,
Fire: melts Earth

BEATS
Earth: is eroded by Air,
Water: is dispersed by Air,

Loses to:
Fire: consumes Air
Lightning: cuts through Air.

BEATS
Water: Lightning overloads Water
Air: Lightning cuts through Air

Loses to:
Earth: absorbs Lightning,
Fire: destabilizes Lightning.

Scoring and Progress

Players earn a score by beating the computer. Every time the player wins the progress bar along the bottom will increase.
Reach 5 wins to fill the progress bar and win the game and your first trophy!
Reach 10 wins to fill the progress bar again and win your second trophy!
Choose the 'hard' play mode for a more challenging game!
