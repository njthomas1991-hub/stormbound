Stormbound

Battle Of The Elements

Rules

Stormbound: A Battle of the Elements is a modern take on Rock, Paper, Scissors.
Choose from: Water , Fire , Earth , Air , Lightning .
After your choice, the computer selects its element after a countdown.

Elements

The winner is determined based on the following rules:

BEATS Fire, Lightning
LOSES TO Air, Earth

BEATS Air, Earth
LOSES TO Water, Lightning

BEATS Lightning, Water
LOSES TO Air, Fire

BEATS Earth, Water
LOSES TO Fire, Lightning

BEATS Water, Air
LOSES TO Earth, Fire

Scoring and Progress

Earn points by beating the computer.
Reach 5 wins → Trophy 1
Reach 10 wins → Trophy 2
Try Hard Mode for a challenge.
